About Spatial Lens

A Product by Geo-Intel Lab • IITTNiF Innovation Hub, Tirupati

Spatial Lens is a geospatial discovery platform for mapping the invisible economy of India — food carts, clinics, kirana stores, panchayat offices, and other everyday places in rural, village, and low-lying areas that rarely appear on conventional maps. From field capture to trusted maps — empowering communities.
